About the author

Takuma Sato, MD, PhD – Reproductive Medicine Specialist

Dr. Sato is a board-certified obstetrician-gynecologist and reproductive medicine specialist based in Tokyo, Japan. He has worked in both university hospitals and IVF clinics, focusing on infertility treatment, IVF/ICSI, and fertility preservation.

In addition to daily clinical practice, he is engaged in research on ovarian reserve, embryo quality and long-term reproductive outcomes, and is passionate about patient education. Through his book, online articles, and social media, he explains complex fertility topics in clear, evidence-based language so that individuals and couples can make informed life and family-planning decisions.
