Hello, I'm Dr. Takuma Sato, a Reproductive Medicine Specialist.

I'm excited to announce the publication of my new book, *"Thinking in your 20s: Choices for a Future Pregnancy Without Trouble"*. In my daily practice, I often encounter patients who express regret, wishing they had known critical information about their fertility sooner. This book was born out of those clinical experiences.

## Why This Book Matters Now
While the internet is full of information regarding the relationship between age and pregnancy rates, as well as male factors, it can be overwhelming to determine what is medically accurate and applicable to your own life.

This book focuses on the following key areas:
1. **The biological mechanism of conception**
2. **Self-care habits to start in your 20s and 30s**
3. **The importance of communication with your partner**

> "I thought pregnancy would just happen naturally when the time was right."

If you share this sentiment, this book is specifically for you.

My hope is that this guide empowers as many people as possible to confidently design their own life plans.
